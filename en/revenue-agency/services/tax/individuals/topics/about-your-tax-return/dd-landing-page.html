Payments the CRA sends you

Direct deposit

You may be looking for:

  • Make a payment to the CRA
 

With direct deposit, you receive your tax refunds and benefit payments faster and securely, directly in your account at a Canadian bank or credit union. In the event of an emergency or unforeseen circumstance, using direct deposit will ensure you continue to get your payments on time.

Services and information

Individuals

Sign up for or change your direct deposit information

Businesses

Update your direct deposit information online or with your Canadian bank or credit union

Trusts

Update your direct deposit information online or by mailing in a form

Non-resident individuals, businesses and trusts

Find the forms to sign up for or change your direct deposit information
