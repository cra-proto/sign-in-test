Cancel authorization for a representative

From: Canada Revenue Agency

Your representative's access to your tax information remains until you or they cancel the access or it expires (if an expiry date was included in the authorization request). Your representative's access will not expire when we are notified of your death, unless they were your legal representative. For more information about legal representatives, go to Legal representative or Legal representative for a deceased person.

Cancel your representative's authorization for a:

  • personal tax account
  • business tax account
  • non-resident tax account
  • trust tax account
Online:

Fastest way. Removing a representative online immediately cancels their access to your CRA account. You may do this through My Account for Individuals.
Cancel using My Account

Alternative online method

Your representative can log in to Represent a Client and cancel their own authorization.

By phone:
Slower way. You or your representative can call to cancel an authorization for your personal account at 1-800-959-8281.

For more information on assistance by telephone including wait times and hours of operation, go to Contact the Canada Revenue Agency.

By mail:
Slowest way. You or your representative can mail a form to the CRA.

Fill in Form AUT-01X, Cancel Authorization for a Representative, and mail it to the appropriate CRA tax centre listed on the form within six months of the date it is signed.

Online:

Fastest way. Removing a representative online immediately cancels their access to your CRA account. You may do this through My Business Account.
Cancel using My Business Account

Alternative online method

Your representative can log in to Represent a Client and cancel their own authorization.

By phone:
Slower way. You or your representative can call to cancel an authorization for your business account at 1-800-959-5525.

For more information on assistance by telephone including wait times and hours of operation, go to Contact the Canada Revenue Agency.

By mail:
Slowest way. You or your representative can mail a form to the CRA.

To cancel your representative's authorization to your:

  • Business account information, fill in Form AUT-01X, Cancel Authorization for a Representative, and mail the form to the appropriate CRA tax centre listed on the form within six months of the date it is signed.
  • Selected Listed Financial Institutions account information, fill in Form RC7259X, Cancel Business Consent or Delegated Authority for Certain Selected Listed Financial Institutions, and mail the form to the CRA tax centre listed on the form within six months of the date it is signed.
By phone:
Fastest way. You or your representative can call to cancel an authorization for your non-resident account.

From Canada and the United States: 1-855-284-5946

From other parts of the world: 613-940-8499

For more information on assistance by telephone including wait times and hours of operation, go to Contact the Canada Revenue Agency.

By mail:
Slowest way. You or your representative can mail a form to the CRA.

Fill in Form AUT-01X, Cancel Authorization for a Representative, and mail it to the following tax centre:

Sudbury Tax Centre
Post Office Box 20000, Station A
Sudbury ON  P3A 5C1
Canada
