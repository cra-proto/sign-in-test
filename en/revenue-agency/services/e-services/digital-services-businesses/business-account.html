CRA Sign in

My Business Account

Sign in or register to view and update your business tax information, file and submit returns for many programs, and access your GST/HST, payroll, corporation income taxes, excise taxes, excise duties and other levies accounts.

Information alerts

A faster way to validate your identity when you register

Interac® document verification service is now available. When you register for a CRA online account, you can use this service to verify your identity immediately instead of waiting 5 to 10 business days to get your CRA security code in the mail.
