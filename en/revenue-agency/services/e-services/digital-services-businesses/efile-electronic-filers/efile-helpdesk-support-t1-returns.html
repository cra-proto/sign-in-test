EFILE Helpdesk support


Contact your designated EFILE Helpdesk at the phone number indicated on your EFILE Renewal/Registration Confirmation page for the following activities:

  • EFILE registration, renewal, account maintenance and/or view transmission history processes;
  • EFILE Suitability Screening and/or Monitoring programs; and 
  • Authentication problems related to EFILE numbers/passwords.
