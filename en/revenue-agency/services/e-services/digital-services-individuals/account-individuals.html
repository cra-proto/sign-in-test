My Account for individuals - CRA Sign in

CRA Sign in

My Account for individuals

Sign in or register to manage your personal and tax information, check the status of your tax refund or benefit payment, make payments to the CRA, change your contact or banking details, and get a notice of assessment or proof of income.

Information alerts

A faster way to validate your identity when you register

Interac® document verification service is now available. When you register for a CRA online account, you can use this service to validate your identity immediately instead of waiting 5 to 10 business days to get your CRA security code in the mail.
