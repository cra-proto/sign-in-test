About Represent a Client

Features for representatives of businesses

The features in Represent a Client (RAC) that are available to representatives on behalf of a business depend on the level of authorization the business has consented to:

Level 1
View information only
Level 2
View and make changes to information
Level 3
View and make changes to information, authorize another representative
Legal representative
To be a legal representative, you must be appointed by a legal document.

Learn more: Authorize a representative: Level of access you can give
