Update your information with the CRA

Inform the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) about changes to your personal, business or charity information. This may include changes to your direct deposit or contact information, such as address, phone number or name changes.

Services and information

Individuals

Change your address, phone number, email, marital status, or name

Businesses

Change of owners, partners or directors, your operating name, address, and more

Charities

Change contact information and details about the director, legal name and status, designation and purposes

Direct deposit

Change direct deposit information for individuals, businesses, trusts, and non-residents
